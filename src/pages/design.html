Maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter "s" on all sides of the logo.

Do

  • Use the logo in approved colors only
  • Maintain minimum clear space at all times
  • Use on high-contrast backgrounds
  • Keep the logo horizontal and unaltered
  • Use the correct variant for light/dark contexts

Don't

  • Stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Add drop shadows, glows, or effects
  • Place on busy photo backgrounds
  • Change the letterforms, spacing, or color
  • Use the light logo on light backgrounds

Do

  • Use stripes as subtle textures or section dividers
  • Apply at low opacity (8–15%) for background accents
  • Use the stamp frame to highlight featured content
  • Pair sand variants with dark backgrounds and vice versa
  • Incorporate in email headers, cards, and social media

Don't

  • Use at full opacity as a primary design element
  • Stretch, distort, or recolor to non-brand colors
  • Use sand variants on light backgrounds
  • Tile or repeat the patterns densely
  • Combine stamp and stripes in the same element
